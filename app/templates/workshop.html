GODOT Workshop: Non-Gregorian Calendar Dates in Digitial Humanities

The first GODOT workshop about non-Gregorian dates in digital environments will be held in Heidelberg on 26. and 27. November 2018 at the Heidelberg Academy of Sciences and Humanities.
